My name is Darius Stroman

I've always been super curious about how far artificial intelligence (AI) can really go. I can't help but wonder, "Will robots eventually take over the world, or will humans manage to stay on top?" As a college student who loves to explore, I've been able to satisfy my curiosity by working on different projects using Python, my go-to programming language. Along with my classes at school, I've taken the initiative to learn more through personal training and online resources. I make it a point to level up my skills every day, learning from online courses, workshops, and teaming up with other like-minded people. It's become a daily habit, and each day brings new chances to get better at what I do. As I get ready to kick off my professional journey, I'm super excited and eager to get started. It's not about the money for me; it's all about how much I truly enjoy AI and the challenges it brings. I can't wait to join others who are just as passionate about shaping the future of AI and use my skills to make a real impact in this awesome field.
